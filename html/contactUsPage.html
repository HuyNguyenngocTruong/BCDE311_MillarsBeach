Volunteer with Us

Want to help us restore Millars Beach? Fill out the volunteer form below and we’ll get in touch.

Role of a Volunteer

  • Assist in regular beach clean-ups and conservation projects
  • Help organize community awareness events
  • Contribute to planting and dune restoration initiatives
  • Support workshops, training days, and eco-education programs

Time Commitment

  • Most volunteer sessions last 2–3 hours
  • Events are usually held on weekends or weekday afternoons
  • We recommend volunteering at least once per month – but you're welcome as often as you're available!
  • Flexible options: One-off, short-term, or long-term volunteering all welcome

No prior experience is needed – just bring your enthusiasm, suitable clothing, and a commitment to care for our natural environment. Volunteers are a vital part of our mission to create a cleaner, greener future for Millars Beach.
